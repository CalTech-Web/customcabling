## FAQ Content

**Q: What areas do you serve?**
We are based in Flowery Branch, Georgia and serve the greater Atlanta metro area including Gainesville, Roswell, Athens, Gwinnett County, and surrounding communities.

**Q: Are you licensed and insured?**
Yes. Custom Cabling Solutions is a low-voltage unrestricted licensed and insured cabling company.

**Q: What industry standards do you follow?**
All of our installations follow BICSI and NEC industry standards to ensure quality, safety, and compliance.

**Q: Do you handle both commercial and residential projects?**
Yes. We specialize in commercial, residential, and underground cabling systems. No project is too large or too small.

**Q: What types of cabling do you install?**
We install structured cabling (Cat5e, Cat6, Cat6a), fiber optic (single-mode and multi-mode), coaxial, and specialty low-voltage cabling for security, audio/video, and phone systems.

**Q: Do you offer free consultations?**
Yes. Contact us to schedule a free on-site or virtual consultation to discuss your cabling needs.
